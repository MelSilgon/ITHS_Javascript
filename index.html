What is public domain?

The public domain (PD) consists of all the creative work to which no exclusive intellectual property rights apply.

Those rights may have expired, been forfeited, expressly waived, or may be inapplicable. Because those rights have expired, anyone can legally use or reference those works without permission. (Wikipedia)
